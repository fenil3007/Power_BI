# Customer Churn Prediction Analysis

## Overview

This Power BI project focuses on analyzing customer behavior, predicting customer churn risk, and evaluating sales performance using interactive dashboards and analytical visualizations.

The report is designed to provide business insights into customer retention, sales trends, product performance, and regional analysis.

---

# Report Pages

## 1. Customer Churn Prediction Analysis

This page focuses on customer churn analytics and risk evaluation.

### Features Included

* Customer churn probability analysis
* Risk category segmentation
* Regional churn analysis
* State and city level drill-down
* Customer behavior insights
* KPI cards for churn metrics
* Interactive slicers and filters
* Dynamic drill-down visualizations

### Key Insights

* Identification of high-risk customers
* Region-wise churn comparison
* Customer distribution by risk category
* Churn trend analysis across locations

---

## 2. Sales Data Analysis

This page focuses on sales performance and business growth analysis.

### Features Included

* Total sales analysis
* Product performance tracking
* Top-selling products
* Regional and city-wise sales breakdown
* Sales trend visualization
* Dynamic ranking and Top-N analysis
* Interactive KPI cards and charts

### Key Insights

* High-performing products and regions
* Sales contribution analysis
* Revenue trend monitoring
* Geographic sales distribution

---

# Tools & Technologies Used
